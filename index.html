    • Info:

    Hyperemesis gravidarum affects approximately 0.3-1% of pregnant women. The condition involves persistent extreme vomiting. It differs from normal pregnancy nausea as it means that the pregnant woman vomits constantly throughout the day. The pregnant woman may vomit up to 15-20 times and usually needs medical care for dehydration. The woman feels smells, tastes and nausea very strongly during all hours of the day.
